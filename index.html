Dark Depths: Idle Dungeon ("the Game") is a free-to-play mobile game. This policy describes how information is collected and used.

The Game does not collect any personal information such as your name, email address, or location. The Game does not require an account or login.

The Game uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers (such as the Android Advertising ID) to serve personalized or non-personalized ads. This data is handled by Google in accordance with their privacy policy.

Data Storage

Game progress (such as level, gold, and hero data) is stored locally on your device only. No game data is transmitted to any server.
